# Modus Tollens (modo que nega)


$$
\begin{gathered}
	p \implies q \\
	\lnot q \\
	\therefore \lnot p
\end{gathered}
$$


| $p$ | $q$ | $p \implies q$ | $\lnot q$ |

|  V  |  F  |       F        |     V     |
|  F  |  V  |       V        |     F     |
|  F  |  F  |       V        |     V     |
