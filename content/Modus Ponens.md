# Modus Ponens (modo que afirma)

$$
\begin{gathered}
	p \implies q \\
	p \\
	\therefore q
\end{gathered}
$$


| $p$ | $q$ | $p \implies q$ |
|:---:|:---:|:--------------:|
|  V  |  V  |       V        | 
|  V  |  F  |       F        |
|  F  |  V  |       V        |
|  F  |  F  |       V        |
